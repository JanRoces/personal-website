My journey into programming began with customizing the front pages of my Xanga and MySpace profiles using HTML. It was the perfect blend of problem-solving and artistic expression, and I was hooked from the start.

Originally from Honolulu, Hawaii, I relocated to Las Vegas, Nevada in 2001. I went on to earn my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Throughout my studies, I taught myself web development and explored graphic design, combining technical skills with visual creativity.

Today, I work as a Software Engineer, where I've led my team through numerous projects and feature launches, bringing both leadership and hands-on technical expertise to the table.
